Fall 2015 - Spring 2016

The Yearbook this year was designed by the GPA Yearbook class. Each student helped to create at least one page spread, gather photos, and vote on design choices. The whole class participated in making this a great Yearbook for 2015-2016. Please congratulate your friends who contributed to this effort (pictured below).

Student Pictures

The first section of the Yearbook shows the GPA class rosters and student pictures. No Yearbook would be complete without these! Thanks to everyone for sending in your selfies and profile photos!

Classes, Activities, Events

Section Two gathers together all the photos from the most memorable classes, activities, and events of the last year - and includes pictures of familiar faces of some of our friends who left in the winter.

Entertainment Survey

The final section of the Yearbook collects the results of our Entertainment survey we took. It was painstakingly crafted (through 3 lunch periods during the Final test week) by Sung Woo Choi.

    Special Features

  1. Year in Review

    It's fun to be able to look back and remember the good times you had and the events that were happening. So this Yearbook includes at least four ways for you to remember 2015-2016.

    1. A timeline of memorable world events
    2. The Vice Principal's letter
    3. The Entertainment/Pop culture survey
    4. Your pictures!

  2. Spring 2016 Graduation

    After the Spring 2016 graduation ceremony, I added a new page to the Yearbook with pictures specifically from that event. It's nice to see all of our graduates giving their speeches as they get ready to head off into the next stage of life.

  3. Highlights of Student work

    There are various sections throughout the Yearbook that show off what YOU have accomplished this past year! From art and science projects, to winning basketball scores, to recitals, photography, yearbook, and putting out fires, you have a lot to be proud of!
